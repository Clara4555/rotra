About Rotra Horizon

Rotra Horizon B.V. is a leading tank storage company committed to providing high-quality storage solutions for various liquid products. With decades of experience since our founding in 1982, we have established ourselves as a reliable partner for businesses across Europe, Asia, and the Americas. Our unwavering commitment to excellence has earned us a reputation for reliability and innovation in the industry.

Our state-of-the-art facilities and strategic locations in key ports around the world enable us to deliver efficient and cost-effective services to our clients, meeting their unique storage needs. Each terminal is equipped with the latest technology in tank monitoring, safety systems, and loading/unloading infrastructure to ensure optimal handling of your products.

At Rotra Horizon, we understand the critical nature of proper storage for different product types, including chemicals, oils, biofuels, and specialty liquids. Our team of highly trained specialists work closely with clients to develop customized storage solutions that meet specific requirements while adhering to the strictest international safety and environmental standards.

Beyond just storage, we offer a comprehensive suite of complementary services including blending, heating, nitrogen blanketing, and specialized handling procedures for sensitive products. Our integrated approach means you can trust Rotra Horizon as your complete tank storage partner.

Our Services

Comprehensive tank storage solutions for your business

At Rotra Horizon B.V., we offer a broad spectrum of integrated tank storage and handling services tailored to meet the complex demands of modern supply chains. Our comprehensive approach ensures that all your liquid product storage and logistics needs are met with the highest standards of efficiency, safety, and environmental responsibility.

Storage Solutions

State-of-the-art tank storage facilities designed for various liquid products with optimal safety standards. Our terminals feature specialized tanks for hazardous and non-hazardous chemicals, petroleum products, biofuels, edible oils, and specialty liquids.

Each storage solution is customized according to product specifications, including temperature control, vapor recovery systems, nitrogen blanketing, and specialized mixing capabilities. Our facilities adhere to all international safety regulations and industry best practices.

Terminal Operations

Efficient loading and unloading services at strategic port locations with excellent connectivity to major shipping routes, rail networks, and road transportation hubs. Our terminals are equipped with advanced loading arms, high-capacity pumps, and automated systems for rapid vessel turnaround.

We provide 24/7 operations with skilled personnel to ensure smooth handling of your products. Our advanced scheduling and coordination systems help minimize waiting times and optimize logistics planning for all transport modes.

Cleaning Services

Professional tank cleaning solutions ensuring the highest standards of cleanliness and safety. Our specialized cleaning teams use environmentally friendly methods and state-of-the-art equipment to thoroughly clean and decontaminate storage tanks, transport containers, and related equipment.

We employ closed-loop cleaning systems that minimize waste and recover solvents whenever possible. All cleaning processes are documented and certified to meet quality assurance requirements and regulatory compliance for subsequent product storage.

Transport

Reliable transportation services to complement our storage solutions with timely delivery. Our transport fleet includes specialized tanker trucks, rail cars, and barge vessels equipped for safe transport of various liquid products, from hazardous chemicals to food-grade materials.

Every transport operation is carefully planned and monitored through advanced tracking systems, ensuring real-time visibility and precise delivery scheduling. Our drivers and operators receive ongoing training in safety protocols, emergency response, and product-specific handling requirements.

Beyond our core services, we offer value-added solutions including product blending, customs clearance assistance, quality control testing, inventory management systems, and specialized handling of temperature-sensitive or high-value products. Our experienced team works closely with clients to create tailored service packages that optimize their supply chain operations.

Why Choose Rotra Horizon

Our commitment to excellence sets us apart in the industry

Safety First

Industry-leading safety protocols and regular audits to ensure the highest level of protection. Our safety management system is ISO 45001 certified and includes comprehensive risk assessments, regular drills, and ongoing training programs.

We maintain a dedicated safety team that conducts routine inspections of all facilities and equipment. Our incident prevention record is among the best in the industry, with continuous improvement processes that incorporate lessons learned and best practices from global operations.

Environmentally Conscious

Sustainable practices and eco-friendly solutions minimizing our environmental footprint. Our facilities incorporate advanced emission control systems, energy-efficient equipment, and water conservation technologies to reduce resource consumption.

We've invested heavily in renewable energy solutions, with solar panels installed at several terminals and plans for wind energy implementation at coastal locations. Our environmental management system is ISO 14001 certified, demonstrating our systematic approach to managing environmental responsibilities.

Advanced Technology

Cutting-edge equipment and monitoring systems for optimal storage conditions and efficiency. Our terminals feature automated inventory tracking, real-time product monitoring, and advanced control systems that provide precise management of all stored products.

We utilize predictive maintenance systems that analyze equipment performance data to identify potential issues before they cause disruptions. Our integrated management platform gives clients secure access to real-time information about their products, including inventory levels, quality parameters, and scheduled movements.

Expert Team

Highly trained professionals dedicated to providing exceptional service and support. Our team includes chemical engineers, logistics specialists, safety experts, and experienced operations personnel who understand the unique requirements of different product types.

We invest heavily in ongoing professional development, with regular training programs that keep our team updated on the latest industry standards and technologies. Many of our key personnel have decades of experience in the tank storage industry, bringing valuable insights and problem-solving capabilities to every client relationship.

Our commitment to excellence extends beyond these key features. We maintain transparent communication with clients, provide flexible service agreements to accommodate changing business needs, and continuously invest in infrastructure improvements to enhance our service capabilities. With Rotra Horizon as your partner, you gain access to a global network of terminals and a team that treats your products with the same care and attention as if they were our own.
