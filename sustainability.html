Our Commitment to Sustainability

Environmental responsibility at the core of our operations

At Rotra Horizon B.V., we recognize our responsibility to minimize the environmental impact of our operations and contribute to a more sustainable future. Sustainability is not just a commitment but an integral part of our business strategy and daily operations.

We are dedicated to implementing innovative solutions that reduce emissions, conserve resources, and promote biodiversity while maintaining the highest standards of operational excellence and service quality. Our comprehensive sustainability approach encompasses energy efficiency, emissions reduction, responsible resource management, and biodiversity protection.

Through continuous improvement, stakeholder engagement, and transparency in reporting, we strive to lead the industry in sustainable tank storage practices, demonstrating that environmental responsibility and business success can go hand in hand.

Our Sustainability Strategy

A comprehensive approach to environmental stewardship

Our sustainability strategy is built on a foundation of scientific understanding, stakeholder engagement, and continuous improvement. We have developed a comprehensive framework that addresses the most significant environmental impacts of our operations while creating long-term value for our business and stakeholders.

This strategy is guided by global sustainability principles, including the UN Sustainable Development Goals, and is regularly reviewed and updated to reflect emerging environmental challenges, technological advancements, and evolving stakeholder expectations. We have established clear targets and key performance indicators to measure our progress and drive accountability throughout our organization.

Our approach is holistic, addressing not only our direct operational impacts but also collaborating with suppliers, customers, and industry partners to promote sustainability throughout the value chain. Through innovation, investment, and dedication, we are committed to leading the transition to a more sustainable tank storage industry.

Energy Efficiency

Reducing energy consumption through innovative technologies, operational improvements, and renewable energy integration.

Key Sustainability Initiatives

Turning commitment into action

Energy & Emissions Management

We are committed to reducing our energy consumption and greenhouse gas emissions through comprehensive initiatives across our operations. Our energy management program focuses on efficiency improvements, renewable energy adoption, and innovative technologies to minimize our carbon footprint.

Key Initiatives:

  • Solar Power Integration: Installation of solar panels at our terminals to generate clean electricity for operations.
  • LED Lighting Retrofit: Conversion to energy-efficient LED lighting throughout our facilities, reducing electricity consumption.
  • Vapor Recovery Systems: Advanced systems to capture and recover volatile organic compounds, reducing emissions and product loss.
  • Energy Monitoring: Real-time energy monitoring systems to identify optimization opportunities and track performance.
  • Pump Efficiency Program: Regular maintenance and upgrades to ensure optimal efficiency of pumping systems.
  • Green Power Purchasing: Procurement of renewable energy through power purchase agreements where feasible.

Water & Waste Management

We are dedicated to responsible water use and waste management across our operations. Our comprehensive approach focuses on reducing water consumption, improving wastewater quality, and minimizing waste generation through innovative solutions and circular economy principles.

Key Initiatives:

  • Water Recycling Systems: Implementation of closed-loop water systems for tank cleaning operations, reducing freshwater consumption.
  • Advanced Wastewater Treatment: State-of-the-art treatment facilities to ensure that discharged water meets or exceeds regulatory standards.
  • Rainwater Harvesting: Collection and use of rainwater for non-potable applications, reducing reliance on municipal water supplies.
  • Waste Segregation Program: Comprehensive waste sorting and segregation to maximize recycling and proper disposal.
  • Sludge Minimization: Technologies and procedures to reduce tank cleaning sludge and maximize resource recovery.
  • Circular Economy Partnerships: Collaboration with specialized partners to find beneficial uses for various waste streams.

Biodiversity Protection

We recognize the importance of protecting and enhancing biodiversity in the areas where we operate. Our biodiversity strategy aims to minimize our ecological footprint, restore natural habitats, and contribute positively to local ecosystems through targeted initiatives and partnerships.

Key Initiatives:

  • Habitat Creation: Development of green spaces and natural habitats within our terminal boundaries to support local wildlife.
  • Pollinator Programs: Installation of pollinator-friendly plantings to support declining bee and butterfly populations.
  • Coastal Protection: Participation in coastal restoration projects near our port facilities to enhance marine habitats.
  • Green Infrastructure: Implementation of green roofs, rainwater gardens, and permeable surfaces to support natural water cycles.
  • Wildlife Monitoring: Regular surveys and monitoring of wildlife populations to track the effectiveness of our initiatives.
  • Community Conservation: Partnerships with local conservation organizations to support broader ecological initiatives.

Sustainable Innovation

Innovation is at the heart of our sustainability strategy. We continuously seek and implement innovative technologies and approaches to reduce our environmental footprint, improve operational efficiency, and develop more sustainable solutions for our customers and the industry as a whole.

Key Initiatives:

  • Smart Terminal Technology: Implementation of IoT sensors and AI analytics to optimize energy use and operational efficiency.
  • Low-Carbon Infrastructure: Investment in low-carbon construction materials and methods for new facility development.
  • Alternative Energy Pilots: Testing of hydrogen, wind, and other renewable energy technologies for terminal operations.
  • Digital Twin Modeling: Advanced digital modeling to optimize terminal design and operations for environmental performance.
  • Green Chemistry: Adoption of environmentally friendly cleaning agents and maintenance chemicals.
  • Innovation Partnerships: Collaboration with universities, startups, and industry partners to develop and test sustainable solutions.

Transparent Reporting & Governance

We are committed to transparency and accountability in our sustainability journey. Through comprehensive reporting and strong governance structures, we ensure that our environmental performance is measured, communicated, and continuously improved.

Our Approach:

  • Annual Sustainability Report: Comprehensive reporting of our environmental performance, initiatives, and progress toward goals.
  • Third-Party Verification: Independent verification of key environmental data to ensure accuracy and credibility.
  • Executive Oversight: Sustainability performance is overseen at the highest levels of our organization, with executive accountability.
  • Stakeholder Engagement: Regular dialogue with stakeholders to understand expectations and receive feedback on our performance.
  • International Standards: Alignment with globally recognized frameworks such as GRI (Global Reporting Initiative) and TCFD (Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures).
  • Continuous Improvement: Regular review and enhancement of our sustainability management systems and reporting practices.
