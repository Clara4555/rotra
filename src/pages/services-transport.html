Integrated Transport Solutions

Connecting your products to global markets

Rotra Horizon B.V. offers comprehensive transport solutions that seamlessly integrate with our storage services, providing end-to-end logistics support for your liquid product supply chain. Our multimodal transport network ensures reliable, efficient, and safe movement of your products to and from our terminals.

With a modern fleet, experienced professionals, and advanced tracking systems, we deliver transportation services that meet the highest standards of reliability, safety, and efficiency. Whether you need road transport, barge services, or intermodal solutions, our team can develop a customized transport plan that aligns with your specific requirements.

Our transport operations are guided by our commitment to safety, sustainability, and customer satisfaction, with a focus on optimizing routes, minimizing emissions, and ensuring timely deliveries.

Our Transport Modes

Multimodal solutions for efficient product movement

Road Transport Services

Our modern fleet of specialized tanker trucks provides flexible and reliable road transport for a wide range of liquid products. With extensive coverage across Europe, we offer efficient door-to-door deliveries with precise scheduling and real-time tracking.

  • Fleet of ADR-compliant tanker trucks
  • Various tank types for different product requirements
  • Temperature-controlled transport options
  • GPS tracking and monitoring systems
  • Experienced drivers with specialized training
  • Flexible scheduling to meet your timing needs
  • Cross-border transport capabilities

Barge Transport Services

For efficient and environmentally friendly bulk transport, our barge services provide cost-effective movement of large volumes along Europe's extensive inland waterway network. With direct access from our terminals to major waterways, we offer seamless barge transport solutions.

  • Access to an extensive network of inland barges
  • Various capacities to suit different volume requirements
  • Lower environmental impact compared to road transport
  • Cost-effective for large volume shipments
  • Specialized equipment for diverse liquid products
  • Scheduled services on major European waterways
  • Integrated terminal-to-terminal solutions

Rail Transport Services

Our rail transport solutions offer efficient movement of larger volumes over long distances. With direct rail connections at our terminals and partnerships with major rail operators, we provide reliable and sustainable rail transport options for your products.

  • Access to tank car fleets across Europe
  • Direct rail connections from our terminals
  • Reduced carbon footprint for long-distance transport
  • Efficient for large volume, long-distance shipments
  • Various tank car types for different products
  • Integration with major European rail networks
  • Track and trace capabilities

Intermodal Transport Solutions

Our integrated intermodal solutions combine the advantages of different transport modes, optimizing your supply chain for efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and environmental performance. We coordinate seamless transfers between transport modes, providing end-to-end logistics support.

  • Coordinated combinations of road, rail, and barge transport
  • Optimized routing based on cost, time, and environmental factors
  • Seamless transfers at intermodal terminals
  • Single point of contact for entire transport chain
  • Reduced carbon footprint through modal optimization
  • Comprehensive tracking across all transport modes
  • Flexible contingency planning

Our Fleet & Equipment

Modern assets for safe and efficient transport

Road Tanker Fleet

Our road transport fleet consists of modern tanker trucks specifically designed for the safe and efficient transport of various liquid products. All vehicles meet the latest safety and emissions standards, with specialized equipment for different product types.

  • Various tank capacities from 20,000 to 40,000 liters
  • Stainless steel and aluminum tank options
  • Multi-compartment configurations available
  • Temperature control capabilities
  • Advanced safety features and monitoring systems
  • Euro 6 compliance for reduced emissions

Barge Equipment

Through our extensive network of partners, we have access to a wide range of specialized barges for inland waterway transport. These vessels offer efficient bulk transport with lower environmental impact compared to road transport for long distances.

  • Capacities ranging from 500 to 3,000 tons
  • Double-hull designs for enhanced safety
  • Specialized equipment for various liquid products
  • Heating capabilities for temperature-sensitive products
  • Modern navigation and monitoring systems
  • Compliance with all safety and environmental regulations

Rail Tank Cars

Our rail transport solutions utilize modern tank cars designed for the safe transport of liquid products over long distances. Through partnerships with major rail operators, we provide access to various types of tank cars suited to different products and volumes.

  • Various capacities to meet different volume requirements
  • Specialized tank designs for different product types
  • Insulated and heated tank cars available
  • Safety features exceeding regulatory requirements
  • Regular maintenance and inspection programs
  • Tracking and monitoring capabilities

Key Features:

Safety & Regulatory Compliance

Prioritizing safe and compliant transport operations

Safety Standards

Our transport operations adhere to the highest safety standards, with comprehensive protocols for loading, transport, and unloading activities.

Regulatory Compliance

We ensure full compliance with all applicable transport regulations, including ADR for road, ADN for inland waterways, and RID for rail transport.

Driver Training

Our drivers undergo extensive training in safe handling of dangerous goods, defensive driving, and emergency response procedures.

Regular Inspections

All transport equipment undergoes rigorous inspections and preventative maintenance to ensure operational safety and reliability.

Health & Safety

We maintain comprehensive health and safety programs for all personnel involved in transport operations, fostering a strong safety culture.

Emergency Response

Our detailed emergency response plans and trained response teams ensure rapid and effective action in the event of an incident.

Sustainable Transport Solutions

At Rotra Horizon, we are committed to reducing the environmental impact of our transport operations. Through innovative solutions and strategic planning, we help our clients achieve more sustainable supply chains while maintaining efficiency and reliability.

Our Sustainable Transport Initiatives:

  • Modern, fuel-efficient vehicles with reduced emissions
  • Modal shift to lower-impact transport modes where feasible
  • Optimized route planning to minimize fuel consumption
  • Driver training in eco-driving techniques
  • Alternative fuel trials and adoption where viable
  • Carbon footprint measurement and reduction strategies
  • Collaboration with industry partners on sustainability innovations

We work closely with our clients to identify opportunities for enhancing the sustainability of their transport operations while meeting their business requirements.
