Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

What types of products can be stored at your facilities?

Our terminals are equipped to store a wide range of liquid products, including petroleum products, chemicals, biofuels, vegetable oils, and other industrial liquids. Each of our terminals has specific capabilities, so please contact us for detailed information about storage options for your particular product.

How do I request a quote for your services?

You can request a quote by filling out the contact form on this page, emailing sales@rotrahorizon.com, or calling our sales team directly at +31 10 123 4570. Please provide as much detail as possible about your storage requirements, including product type, volume, and timeframe, to help us prepare an accurate quote.

What safety measures do you have in place at your terminals?

Safety is our top priority. Our terminals feature comprehensive safety systems including fire detection and suppression, gas detection, emergency shutdown capabilities, spill containment, and 24/7 monitoring. We maintain rigorous safety protocols, conduct regular drills, and invest in ongoing training for all personnel. All our facilities comply with or exceed applicable safety regulations.

Do you offer both short-term and long-term storage options?

Yes, we offer flexible storage arrangements to meet different business needs. We provide both short-term storage for operational flexibility and long-term contracts for supply chain stability. Our team can work with you to develop a customized solution that aligns with your specific requirements and timeframes.

How do you handle environmental compliance?

We are committed to environmental responsibility and maintain comprehensive environmental management systems certified to ISO 14001 standards. Our facilities incorporate advanced emission control technologies, water treatment systems, and waste management practices. We comply with all applicable environmental regulations and regularly undergo third-party audits to verify our performance.
