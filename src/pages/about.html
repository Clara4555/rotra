Our History

Rotra Horizon B.V. was established in 1985 with a vision to provide high-quality tank storage solutions for businesses around the world. What began as a small operation with a few storage tanks has grown into a leading provider in the industry.

Over the decades, we have expanded our operations across strategic locations, continuously improving our infrastructure and adopting the latest technologies to enhance our services. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has been at the heart of our growth and success.

Today, Rotra Horizon is recognized for its reliability, safety standards, and innovative approach to tank storage solutions. We continue to evolve and adapt to the changing needs of our clients and the industry, while staying true to our founding principles.

1985

Foundation

Rotra Horizon B.V. was established in Rotterdam

1995

First Expansion

Expanded capacity to 500,000 cubic meters

2002

International Growth

Established first international terminal to expand our services beyond domestic boundaries. This strategic expansion allowed us to better serve multinational clients with cross-border supply chain needs.

Our first international facility in Antwerp became the foundation for our European network, designed to international standards and equipped with state-of-the-art technology. This milestone marked our transition from a national operator to a key player in the European tank storage market.

2010

Sustainable Initiative

Launched comprehensive sustainability program that fundamentally transformed our approach to environmental responsibility. This initiative went beyond compliance to position Rotra Horizon as an industry pioneer in sustainable practices.

The program included significant investments in renewable energy systems, waste reduction protocols, and water conservation technologies. We also implemented rigorous environmental monitoring systems and partnered with environmental organizations to restore natural habitats near our facilities.

This sustainability framework became the foundation for all future expansions and operational decisions, ensuring environmental considerations are integrated into every aspect of our business.

Today

Industry Leader

Recognized as a global leader in tank storage solutions with operations spanning three continents and serving more than 500 clients worldwide. Our network now encompasses 12 strategic terminals with a combined capacity exceeding 5 million cubic meters.

Our industry leadership extends beyond size to innovation, having pioneered several technological breakthroughs in storage efficiency and safety protocols. Our terminals consistently exceed regulatory requirements, setting new benchmarks for operational excellence in every region where we operate.
