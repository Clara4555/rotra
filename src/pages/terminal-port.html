Our Terminal Network

Strategic locations offering efficient storage and distribution solutions

Rotra Horizon B.V. operates a network of state-of-the-art terminals strategically located in key port areas across Europe. Our facilities are designed to provide efficient, safe, and reliable tank storage solutions for a wide range of liquid products.

With deep-water access, excellent hinterland connections, and advanced infrastructure, our terminals serve as crucial links in the global supply chain, enabling seamless product movement between sea, rail, road, and pipeline networks.

Each of our terminals adheres to the highest standards of safety, efficiency, and environmental protection, equipped with modern technology and operated by experienced professionals committed to delivering exceptional service to our clients.

Our Strategic Locations

Serving global markets from key port locations

Rotra Horizon B.V. has carefully selected our terminal locations to optimize global supply chain efficiency and provide strategic advantages for our clients. Our presence in major European port cities creates a robust network that facilitates seamless product movement across international markets. With over 1.4 million cubic meters of total storage capacity across four strategically positioned terminals, we offer unparalleled logistics solutions for the liquid bulk industry.

Each terminal is designed with specific regional requirements in mind, while maintaining our consistent high standards for safety, efficiency, and environmental protection. Our facilities are equipped with modern infrastructure that accommodates various vessel types, product specifications, and handling requirements, providing versatile solutions for diverse client needs. All terminals operate under integrated management systems certified to ISO 9001, ISO 14001, and ISO 45001 standards, ensuring consistent quality, environmental performance, and safety across our entire network.

The strategic positioning of our terminals offers significant logistical advantages, including intermodal connectivity, access to major shipping routes, and proximity to key industrial centers. This network approach allows us to provide comprehensive storage and distribution services that support our clients' global operations. Our terminals serve as critical nodes in international supply chains, connecting producers with consumers across Europe, the Mediterranean basin, and beyond through efficient maritime and inland transportation links.

Our commitment to continuous improvement drives ongoing investment in terminal infrastructure and technology, ensuring that our facilities remain at the forefront of the industry in terms of operational efficiency, safety performance, and environmental protection. Each expansion or upgrade is planned with careful consideration of long-term market trends and evolving client requirements, allowing us to anticipate and respond effectively to changing industry dynamics.

Rotterdam Terminal

Our flagship terminal in the Port of Rotterdam, Europe's largest port, offering excellent connectivity to European markets. This state-of-the-art facility serves as our operational headquarters and represents the pinnacle of our technical capabilities and service offerings.

Strategically positioned in the Europoort area with direct access to the North Sea, our Rotterdam terminal provides unparalleled accessibility for vessels of all sizes, from coastal barges to the largest ocean-going tankers. The terminal's deep-water berths can accommodate vessels with drafts up to 15 meters, enabling efficient cargo transfers for global shipping operations.

Established in 1998 and significantly expanded in 2015, this facility features cutting-edge automation systems, energy-efficient operations, and comprehensive safety features that exceed industry standards. With excellent intermodal connections via road, rail, and inland waterways, the Rotterdam terminal serves as a crucial logistics hub for clients throughout Northwestern Europe.

  • 15m draft capacity
  • 500,000 m続 storage capacity
  • 4 deep-sea berths
  • Direct rail connection

Antwerp Terminal

Strategically located in Europe's second-largest port, providing access to the European heartland. Our Antwerp facility serves as a critical connection point between maritime shipping routes and the extensive network of inland waterways, railways, and highways that penetrate deep into continental Europe.

Situated in the heart of the Antwerp Chemical Cluster, this terminal specializes in handling specialized chemical products and offers dedicated facilities for hazardous materials management. The location benefits from proximity to major chemical manufacturers, creating a seamless supply chain for our industrial clients throughout the Benelux region, Germany, and Northern France.

Commissioned in 2005 and expanded in 2018, the Antwerp terminal incorporates cutting-edge safety systems designed specifically for chemical storage, including advanced vapor treatment systems, specialized firefighting equipment, and comprehensive environmental monitoring. The facility's modern control center operates 24/7, providing real-time inventory management and operational oversight to ensure maximum efficiency and reliability.

  • 13m draft capacity
  • 350,000 m続 storage capacity
  • 3 deep-sea berths
  • Excellent road connections

Hamburg Terminal

Serving the Baltic region and Northern European markets from Germany's largest port. Our Hamburg terminal provides a strategic gateway to Scandinavia, the Baltic states, and Eastern European markets, making it an essential component of our European terminal network.

This facility specializes in handling petroleum products and biofuels, with dedicated infrastructure designed to support Germany's energy transition. The terminal features specialized equipment for renewable fuel handling, including segregated storage options for various biofuel grades and advanced quality control systems to ensure product integrity throughout the storage and handling process.

Established in 2010, the Hamburg terminal represents our newest major European facility and incorporates the latest technological innovations in terminal operations. The terminal's advanced automation systems minimize manual interventions, enhance operational efficiency, and reduce the potential for human error, ensuring consistent service quality while optimizing resource utilization across all operations.

  • 12m draft capacity
  • 250,000 m続 storage capacity
  • 2 deep-sea berths
  • Pipeline connections

Marseille Terminal

Our Mediterranean hub providing access to Southern European and North African markets. The Marseille terminal serves as our strategic gateway to the Mediterranean basin, offering direct maritime connections to North Africa, the Middle East, and Southern European markets.

The facility specializes in petroleum product distribution across the Mediterranean region and features dedicated infrastructure for jet fuel, diesel, and gasoline storage and handling. With its deep-water capabilities, the terminal can accommodate large tankers that serve long-distance Mediterranean shipping routes, making it an important transshipment hub for our global operations.

Acquired in 2012 and extensively modernized in 2020, the Marseille terminal incorporates innovative environmental protection systems designed specifically for sensitive Mediterranean coastal ecosystems. The terminal operates under an integrated environmental management plan that includes continuous water quality monitoring, enhanced spill prevention protocols, and specialized marine ecosystem protection measures in accordance with Mediterranean environmental regulations.

  • 14m draft capacity
  • 300,000 m続 storage capacity
  • 3 deep-sea berths
  • Proximity to industrial zones

Terminal Features & Capabilities

Advanced infrastructure for efficient and safe operations

Advanced Storage Facilities

Our terminals feature state-of-the-art storage tanks designed to handle a wide range of liquid products safely and efficiently. With various tank sizes and specifications, we can accommodate diverse storage needs while maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety.

Our storage infrastructure includes both fixed-roof and floating-roof tanks constructed with cutting-edge materials and engineering designs that meet or exceed international standards. Each tank is equipped with advanced monitoring systems that provide real-time data on product levels, temperature, pressure, and other critical parameters, ensuring optimal storage conditions and early detection of any operational anomalies.

The tank farms are arranged in strategic configurations that maximize operational efficiency while ensuring complete segregation between different product types. Dedicated piping systems prevent cross-contamination and maintain product purity throughout the storage process, which is especially important for sensitive products like specialty chemicals and food-grade ingredients.

  • Carbon steel and stainless steel tanks
  • Pressurized and atmospheric storage options
  • Temperature-controlled tanks
  • Nitrogen blanketing capabilities
  • Automated level monitoring systems
  • Vapor recovery units
  • Secondary containment systems

Marine Infrastructure

Our terminals feature deep-water jetties and berths capable of accommodating vessels of various sizes, from coastal barges to large ocean-going tankers. With modern loading arms, high-capacity pumps, and efficient mooring systems, we ensure quick and safe vessel turnaround times.

Each terminal's marine facilities are designed and maintained to the highest international standards, ensuring safe and efficient vessel operations in all weather conditions. Our Rotterdam and Marseille terminals are equipped with specialized all-weather loading systems that allow continuous operations even during adverse weather conditions, minimizing costly delays for vessel operators.

Our marine infrastructure includes state-of-the-art berthing assistance systems, featuring advanced fender designs, laser-guided docking systems, and specialized mooring equipment that enhance safety during vessel arrival and departure. Digital monitoring of berthing operations provides real-time information to vessel operators and terminal personnel, creating a transparent and coordinated approach to marine activities.

  • Multiple deep-water berths at each terminal
  • High-capacity marine loading arms
  • Advanced mooring systems
  • Flexible hose connections
  • Vessel traffic management systems
  • 24/7 marine operations
  • Experienced marine personnel

Product Handling Capabilities

Our terminals are equipped with advanced product handling systems designed to ensure efficient, safe, and contamination-free transfer of various liquid products. With high-capacity pumps, flexible piping networks, and sophisticated metering systems, we provide reliable product movement services.

We've invested in specialized handling equipment for different product categories, ensuring that each type of liquid is processed according to its specific requirements. For sensitive products, we utilize dedicated lines with specialized materials that prevent contamination and maintain product integrity throughout the handling process. Our Hamburg terminal features fully segregated systems for biofuels, preventing any cross-contamination with petroleum products.

Our product handling infrastructure incorporates advanced flow control systems that allow precise regulation of transfer rates, minimizing the risk of pressure surges and ensuring smooth operations even with challenging products. Digital flow management integrates with our inventory systems to provide real-time tracking of all product movements, enhancing both operational efficiency and traceability throughout the supply chain.

  • High-capacity transfer pumps
  • Flexible piping networks
  • Advanced filtration systems
  • Precise metering equipment
  • Blending capabilities
  • Heating and cooling facilities
  • Multiple loading/unloading positions

Comprehensive Safety Systems

Safety is our top priority. Our terminals are equipped with advanced safety systems and follow stringent protocols to prevent incidents and ensure rapid response in emergency situations. Regular drills, training, and audits help maintain our high safety standards.

Our integrated safety management system combines cutting-edge technology with rigorous operational procedures to create multiple layers of protection. The central control rooms at each terminal feature advanced monitoring systems that provide constant oversight of all critical parameters, allowing for early detection of potential issues and rapid implementation of preventive measures before incidents can occur.

We've implemented industry-leading fire protection systems across all terminals, including sophisticated foam-based suppression systems specifically designed for hydrocarbon fires, water deluge systems, and specialized firefighting equipment for chemical products. Our Rotterdam terminal features a dedicated fire brigade with specialized training in industrial firefighting, capable of responding to any emergency within minutes.

Environmental protection is integrated into our safety approach, with comprehensive spill prevention and containment systems including secondary and tertiary containment structures, automated emergency shutdown valves, and specialized spill response equipment stationed throughout each terminal. Regular environmental monitoring ensures that all operations maintain compliance with stringent European environmental regulations.

  • Automated fire detection and suppression systems
  • Gas detection systems
  • Emergency shutdown capabilities
  • Spill containment and recovery equipment
  • 24/7 security monitoring
  • On-site emergency response teams
  • Regular safety drills and exercises
