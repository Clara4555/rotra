Professional Tank Cleaning Solutions

Ensuring the highest standards of cleanliness and safety

At Rotra Horizon B.V., we understand that proper tank cleaning is essential for maintaining product quality, ensuring operational efficiency, and meeting regulatory requirements. Our professional tank cleaning services are designed to deliver thorough, safe, and environmentally responsible cleaning solutions for all types of storage tanks.

With state-of-the-art equipment, specialized cleaning techniques, and a highly trained team, we provide comprehensive cleaning services that meet the highest industry standards. Whether you need routine maintenance cleaning, product change preparation, or emergency cleaning services, our expert team delivers exceptional results while prioritizing safety and environmental protection.

Our cleaning processes are carefully designed to minimize waste, reduce emissions, and ensure the responsible handling of cleaning residues, adhering to strict environmental regulations and our own sustainability commitments.

Our Cleaning Process

A methodical approach for optimal results

01

Initial Assessment

We conduct a thorough assessment of your tank, considering factors such as tank type, size, previous contents, and specific cleaning requirements.

02

Safety Preparation

We implement comprehensive safety measures, including gas testing, ventilation setup, and personal protective equipment deployment.

03

Product Removal

Residual product is carefully removed from the tank, minimizing waste and ensuring proper handling of all materials.

04

Cleaning Execution

Using specialized equipment and appropriate cleaning agents, we execute the cleaning plan, adapting techniques based on tank conditions.

05

Waste Management

All cleaning residues and waste materials are collected, processed, and disposed of in compliance with environmental regulations.

06

Quality Verification

We perform thorough inspections and testing to verify cleanliness standards have been met according to requirements.

07

Documentation & Handover

Comprehensive documentation is provided, including cleaning certificates and verification reports for your records.

Advanced Cleaning Equipment

State-of-the-art technology for superior results

Automated Tank Cleaning Systems

Our programmable robotic cleaning systems enable efficient and thorough cleaning while minimizing the need for personnel entry into confined spaces. These advanced systems use rotating high-pressure spray heads to deliver powerful cleaning action throughout the tank interior.

  • Reduced confined space entry requirements
  • Consistent and thorough cleaning performance
  • Faster cleaning cycle times
  • Lower water and cleaning agent consumption

High-Pressure Washing Equipment

Our industrial-grade high-pressure washing systems deliver powerful cleaning performance for stubborn residues and deposits. With adjustable pressure settings and specialized nozzles, we can customize the cleaning approach for different tank surfaces and contaminants.

  • Pressures up to 3000 bar for challenging deposits
  • Specialized nozzles for different applications
  • Hot water capabilities for improved cleaning efficacy
  • Water recycling systems to reduce consumption

Vacuum Recovery Systems

Our powerful vacuum systems efficiently remove liquids, sludge, and solids from tanks, enabling proper waste segregation and handling. These systems minimize manual handling and improve the overall safety and efficiency of the cleaning operation.

  • High-capacity suction for rapid waste removal
  • Capable of handling various viscosities and solid content
  • Integrated filtration for waste separation
  • Explosion-proof designs for hazardous environments
