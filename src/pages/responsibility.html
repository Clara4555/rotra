Our Approach to Responsibility

Committed to making a positive impact through responsible business practices

At Rotra Horizon B.V., we understand that our operations have an impact on society and the environment. We are committed to conducting our business responsibly, with a focus on creating sustainable value for all our stakeholders, including customers, employees, communities, and the environment.

Our corporate responsibility framework encompasses safety, environmental stewardship, ethical business practices, employee well-being, and community engagement. These pillars guide our decision-making processes and operational practices across all our terminals and facilities.

We continuously strive to improve our performance in these areas, setting ambitious targets, implementing robust management systems, and regularly reporting on our progress. Our commitment to responsibility is integral to our corporate strategy and central to our long-term success.

Our Responsibility Pillars

The key areas that define our approach to corporate responsibility

Safety First

We prioritize the safety and well-being of our employees, contractors, and communities. Our comprehensive safety management system includes regular training, risk assessments, and continuous improvement initiatives to prevent incidents and ensure safe operations.

Environmental Stewardship

We are committed to minimizing our environmental footprint through efficient resource use, waste reduction, and pollution prevention. Our environmental management system helps us monitor and improve our performance in areas such as emissions, energy consumption, and water usage.

Ethical Business Practices

We conduct our business with integrity, transparency, and respect for human rights. Our code of conduct and ethics policies guide our employees and business partners in maintaining high ethical standards in all our operations and relationships.

Employee Well-being

Our employees are our greatest asset. We foster a diverse, inclusive, and supportive workplace culture that enables personal and professional growth. We invest in training, development, and well-being programs to help our employees reach their full potential.

Community Engagement

We actively engage with the communities where we operate, supporting local initiatives, creating employment opportunities, and contributing to social and economic development. We build strong relationships based on open dialogue and mutual respect.

Ethics & Governance

At Rotra Horizon, we believe that conducting business ethically is not just the right thing to do â€“ it's essential for building trust with our stakeholders and ensuring the long-term success of our company.

Our ethical framework is guided by:

  • A comprehensive Code of Business Conduct that outlines our expectations for ethical behavior
  • Anti-corruption and anti-bribery policies and training
